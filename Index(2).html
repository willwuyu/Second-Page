What's on for the internationals?

Areola/Kimpembe/Mbapp?- FRANCE Les Bleus play their final UEFA Nations League game on Friday at the De Kuip stadium in Rotterdam against the Netherlands with first place in the group at stake. They will then face Edinson Cavani at the Stade de France in a friendly next Tuesday.

Kehrer/Draxler - GERMANY

The Parisian duo have a big Nations League game against the Dutch on Monday - a game the 2014 world champions must win to not finish bottom of Group A - which follows Thursday's friendly with Russia. Verratti - ITALY Portugal are Italy's visitors for Saturday's Nations League game. La Squadra Azzura are two points behind their opponents having played a game more. Marco Verratti will then face Timothy Weah and the USA in a friendly on Tuesday. Meunier - BELGLIUM There are two Nations League matches for Thomas Meunier: against Iceland on Thursday and Switzerland on Sunday. The Red Devils are joint-top of their group with the Swiss, but have a game in hand. Cavani - URUGUAY The Celeste have two prestigious friendlies. The first against the Brazil of Marquinhos and Neymar Jr at the Emirates Stadium in London on Friday before the match at the Stade de France on Tuesday.
